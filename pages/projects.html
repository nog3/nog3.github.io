Vendo

Vendo is an ambitious conversion of an old Wurlitzer Vending Machine for HSBNE Inc, the Brisbane Hackerspace. Unfortunately the original coin mechanism setup was faulty and has now been replaced with:
  • A recycled 32 channel relay board
  • An Arduino Mega to control the hardware
  • A Raspberry Pi and Touchscreen TFT for interfacing
  • A serial RFID reader
  • A Node.JS app handling the interface and RFID
